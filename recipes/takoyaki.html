Takoyaki

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 60g all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 180ml dashi
  • 3g baking powder
  • Japanese Mayonnaise
  • Tonkatsu sauce
  • Bonito flakes
  • Dried seaweed flakes
  • Cooked and diced octopus
  • Chopped spring onion
  • Soy sauce

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour with baking powder
  2. Add egg, soy sauce, and dashi to the bowl. Whisk well to combine.
  3. Heat the Takoyaki pan to 200 °C (400 °F) over medium heat. Apply a generous coating of oil to the Takoyaki pan using a brush. Once preheated, pour the batter to fill the holes in the pan
  4. Add one to three octopus pieces into each hole. Sprinkle the top with the bonito flakes and spring onion
  5. Cook for three minutes then rotate each ball 90 degrees using skewers, stuffing in any edges that overflowed onto the pan. Cook for four more minutes. Continue to turn the balls to create a rounded shape
  6. Transfer the balls onto a plate. Drizzle with Tonkatsu sauce, mayonnaise, and a sprinkling of powder seaweed and bonito. Serve immediately.
